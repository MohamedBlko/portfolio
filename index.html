About Me

I like understanding how systems work, then making them clearer.

My interest in engineering comes from building, testing, fixing, and asking why a system behaves the way it does. Electrical engineering fits that curiosity because it connects invisible signals to real-world movement, power, data, and control.

I am especially drawn to work where hardware and software meet: embedded systems, signal behavior, electrical documentation, automation, and test plans. My goal is to keep building projects that show not just the result, but the engineering thinking behind it.
